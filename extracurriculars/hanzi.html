I Got One

I Got One 自圆其说 is an organization aimed to help individuals in memorizing how to write/read Hanzi. For each character design, we utilize strategies shared by others to draw inspiration. Click on the picture for the link to the website or scroll to see my creations.

"This character looks like a 鱼, the top part is the tail, and the square is the body. It seems like the fish is jumping into a pond." - Dome Vivanco

"Think of 肉 as two pieces of meat on a platter" - Will Dreesen

"The character 冬 looks like a person with a muffler in winter" - Yuni Song

"果 looks like an upside down pineapple, which is a fruit" - Luciana Emerson

"The bottom is the bottle and the top part is where you would push down, 香 looks like a perfume bottle" - Luciana Emerson

"The walking radical signifies walking, which is necessary for transport for 运" - Ariana Fahl

"All together the character 慢 looks like a turtle walking which is really slow on land" - Crystal Lin

"友 looks like someone who is cuddled up or feeling small, is protected by his friend" - Sacintha Pilapitiya

"心 looks like a heart" - Varya Obriadova

"另 has a mouth where the head would be and looks like a body underneath, and looks like an alien, like another kind“ - Kat Flanigen

"豆 resembles a small square of tofu in a bowl with "folded" chhopsticks on top" - Victoria Quory

"The silk radical reminds me of water drops and this character sounds like how you would say "rain" in cantonese" - Gloria Tsang

"白 reminds me of an apple, and when I think of an apple, I think of Snow White" - Louise Simpson
